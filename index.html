Hi! I'm an astronomer at the INAF astronomical observatory of Trieste.

I study near-pristine environments, searching for the chemical signature of the first stars. More generally, I am interested in the metal-poor Universe. Part of my research involves using the largest optical telescopes in the world, sometimes using the combined light from all four UTs that make up the VLT. To see my publications, please click here.
